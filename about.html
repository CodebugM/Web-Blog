Philosophy

In modern society the world wide web plays a vital role in commerce, government, research, education, medicine and many other aspects of our lives. In light of this web professionals have an impact on society and thus need to ensure that this impact is positive and beneficial to the customers they serve. To me the most important principles when designing a website are:

  • Merge beauty and function
  • Make website accessible to everyone, incl. people using screenreaders etc.
  • Create ethical websites with verified hate-free content
  • Be responsive to my clients and in turn allow my clients to be responsive to theirs
  • Put people before code
  • Build robust websites before jumping into agile frameworks
  • Use tech for the greater good
